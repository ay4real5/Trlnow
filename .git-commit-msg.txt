fix(admin): route edits now offer to rebuild the timeline; always-visible timeline controls

Two real problems from live use:

1. Editing Origin/Destination in Shipment Details correctly saved the
   new route but never touched the tracking timeline, so a shipment
   changed from London to Hamburg still showed "London Hub" in its
   history with no indication anything was out of sync. Saving a route
   change now auto-generates a suggested journey for the NEW route,
   defaults "replace existing timeline" on, and shows it right there
   with a clear banner - one click to bring history in sync, or discard
   to leave old history alone.
2. Timeline entry edit/delete buttons were hover-only (opacity-0 until
   group-hover), which is invisible on touch devices (no hover state)
   and easy to miss on desktop - exactly the "not giving me control"
   complaint. They're always visible now.

Also: city/country are now display-capitalized ("hamburg" -> "Hamburg")
without touching the stored data, since free-typed casing was showing
up inconsistently next to properly-cased country names.
